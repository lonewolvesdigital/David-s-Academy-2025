David's Academy International cultivates a nurturing and intellectually stimulating environment where students of diverse backgrounds embark on a path towards academic excellence and global citizenship.

From humble beginnings...

Established in 2023 in the heart of Kinshasa, we provide a multi-cultural learning experience with French and English languages classes. Our dedicated team is committed to fostering curiosity, creativity, and a love of learning in children Ansd 18 months to 6 years old.

Mission Statement: To inspire young learners to reach their full potential through a bilingual, multicultural education that fosters curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Vision Statement: To be a leading international school in Kinshasa, recognized for excellence in education, innovation, and our commitment to nurturing global citizens. These statements highlight the school's focus on bilingualism, multiculturalism, and providing a high-quality education that prepares students for success in a globalized world.
