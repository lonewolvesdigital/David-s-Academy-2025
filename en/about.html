History
A Legacy of Education

David's Academy International is rooted in a rich tradition of education. Our founder, Grace Beaud, comes from a family with a deep-seated commitment to nurturing young minds. As the daughter of the founders of Joyeux Lutin in Socimat Kinshasa, Grace Beaudhas a lifelong passion for education.

Grace's journey in education has led her to successfully establish multiple schools including Complexe Scolaire Les Petits Lutins Kintambo (since 2015) and Ma Champagne (2023). Her dedication to providing quality education to children in Africa is unwavering.
